# Privacy Policy for Ride Share Hub

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

## Introduction

Ride Share Hub ("we", "our", or "us") operates a ride-sharing platform connecting passengers with drivers across West Bengal, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
- **Driver Information**: License number, vehicle details, registration documents, Aadhaar/ID verification
- **Location Data**: Real-time GPS location during rides, pickup/drop-off locations
- **Payment Information**: Payment methods, transaction history (processed through secure third-party payment gateways)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features accessed, ride history, search queries
- **Location History**: Previous ride locations for service improvement

### Communications
- **Messages**: In-app communications between drivers and passengers
- **Support Requests**: Customer service correspondence
- **Notifications**: Push notification preferences and interactions

## How We Use Your Information

### Service Delivery
- Match passengers with nearby drivers
- Calculate fares and process payments
- Navigate routes and provide ETAs
- Send ride confirmations and updates

### Safety & Security
- Verify driver identities and vehicle information
- Monitor for fraudulent activities
- Respond to incidents and emergencies
- Comply with legal obligations

### Improvement & Analytics
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
- Personalize user experience
- Develop new features
- Conduct research and analytics

### Communications
- Send booking confirmations and ride updates
- Provide customer support
- Send promotional offers (with your consent)
- Notify about service changes

## Information Sharing

### We Share Information With:

**Drivers and Passengers**
- Basic profile information for matched rides
- Contact details for ride coordination
- Pickup and drop-off locations

**Service Providers**
- Payment processors for transaction handling
- SMS/notification services
- Cloud hosting providers
- Analytics services
- Map and navigation services (OpenStreetMap)

**Legal Requirements**
- Law enforcement when required by law
- Government authorities as mandated
- Legal proceedings and dispute resolution

**Business Transfers**
- In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your location data for advertising
- Provide your contact details to other users without your consent

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure storage of sensitive information
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure payment processing

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Location Data

### How We Use Location
- **Active Rides**: Real-time tracking for navigation and safety
- **Ride Matching**: Finding nearby drivers
- **Service Areas**: Ensuring service availability in West Bengal
- **Route Optimization**: Calculating efficient routes

### Your Controls
- Grant or deny location permissions in device settings
- Enable location only when using the app
- View and delete location history in app settings

## Your Rights

### Access & Control
- **View**: Access your personal information
- **Update**: Correct inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Request account deletion
- **Export**: Download your data
- **Opt-out**: Marketing communications

### How to Exercise Rights
Contact us at: support@ridesharehub.com or through in-app settings

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Ride History**: 7 years for legal and tax compliance
- **Deleted Accounts**: 90 days for account recovery, then permanently deleted
- **Legal Hold**: Data retained longer if required by law

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us immediately.

## Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies:
- **Essential**: For app functionality
- **Analytics**: To understand usage patterns
- **Preferences**: Remember your settings

You can control cookies through browser settings, but this may affect app functionality.

## Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party services (payment gateways, map providers). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in India. If transferred internationally, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place.
